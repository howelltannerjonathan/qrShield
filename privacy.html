qrShield Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Thank you for using qrShield. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information qrShield processes, how it is used, and what information is not collected.

Information We Process

When you scan a QR code, qrShield analyzes the destination URL to help determine whether it may be safe to visit.

To perform this analysis, the scanned URL may be transmitted to trusted third-party security services including:

  • VirusTotal
  • URLhaus

These services analyze the submitted URL and return reputation information that is displayed within the application.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location
  • Photos
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Payment Information
  • Advertising Identifiers

qrShield does not require users to create an account.

Camera Permission

Camera access is used solely to scan QR codes. Camera images are not stored or uploaded.

Data Storage

qrShield does not maintain user accounts or permanently store scanned URLs. URLs are processed only for the duration necessary to complete a security analysis.

Third-Party Services

Security analysis is provided using trusted third-party services including:

  • VirusTotal
  • URLhaus

These services process submitted URLs according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

qrShield is not directed toward children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Effective Date above will always reflect the latest version.
